About Us

Founded and registered under laws of the Republic of South Africa in 2015, Combe 90 Engineering (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black owned and certified level 1 (100% procurement recognition) accreditation on, Broad Based Black Empowerment Enterprise (BBBEE).

Combe 90 Engineering has developed basic and detailed engineering, design and project management strengths to provide engineered, and sustainable telemetry and fuel management solutions to its clients.

Our focus is on providing sound and reliable solutions to the water and petroleum sectors throughout the African continent. This includes application-specific, customized solutions with our product range, to suit client requirements -and all aspects of installation and maintenance thereof.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide cost-effective and efficient telemetry, tank gauging and leak detection solutions for small and medium-sized applications in the commercial, industrial, and municipal domains, while maintaining a professional approach to service delivery required for ensuring proper governance, control, and customer satisfaction.

Our customer-centric business model focuses on the delivery of custom and off-the-shelf products and solutions with backup from reputable suppliers where applicable.
